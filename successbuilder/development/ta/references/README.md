## 🗺️ WA 09-Telecom Administrator Exam Blueprint

This is an **open-book exam** administered by PSI. You must pass **each section separately** with **70% or greater**.

### Exam Structure

| Section | Time Allowed | Questions | Passing Score |
|---|---|---|---|
| **NEC & Theory** | 2 hours | **30 questions** | ≥ 70% |
| **WA Codes** (RCW & WAC) | 1 hour | **17 questions** | ≥ 70% |
| **Total** | 3 hours | **47 questions** | Both sections ≥ 70% |

### ⚠️ Priority Callouts

> **NEC 800-830 (Communication Systems) carries the most NEC points — 8 of 30 questions.** This is the domain-specific core of the exam. Prioritize Articles 800, 805, 810, 820, and 830 above all other NEC sections.

> **WA Laws & Rules (RCW & WAC) is worth more than any single NEC section — 17 of 47 total questions.** Study RCW 19.28 and WAC 296-46B thoroughly alongside the NEC.

### Study Priority Order

| Priority | Section | Questions | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🥇 Critical | NEC 800-830 Communications Systems | 8 | Domain-specific core |
| 🥇 Critical | WA Laws & Rules (RCW & WAC) | 17 | Largest single section |
| 🥈 High | General Trade Knowledge & Theory | 6 | Broad, requires breadth |
| 🥈 High | NEC 705-780 Special Conditions | 4 | Often overlooked |
| 🥉 Medium | NEC 90, 100-110, 250, 300-398 | 2-3 each | Foundational code |
| ⬇️ Low | All remaining articles | ~0-1 each | In scope; low weight |

## 📡 Chapter 8 — Communications Systems (Telco Core)

**Chapter 8 of NEC is the primary chapter for telecommunications administrators.** It operates independently from Chapters 1–7 except where explicitly referenced. It accounts for **8 of 30 NEC questions** on the WA 09 exam — more than any other single article group.

### Article 800 — General Requirements for Communications Systems

Article 800 covers general requirements common across all Chapter 8 articles, including grounding, bonding, listing requirements, and installation of communications cables (twisted-pair, coaxial, fiber). **High-priority for the exam.**

- 🔗 [Nassau National Cable — Article 800 Explained](https://nassaunationalcable.com/blogs/blog/explaining-nec-article-800-on-communication-circuits) — Plain-language breakdown of Article 800 (free)
- 🔗 [EC&M — Article 800: Communications Circuits](https://www.ecmweb.com/national-electrical-code/code-basics/article/20890128/article-800-communications-circuits) — Code basics overview
- 🔗 [EC&M — Article 800 Grounding Rules](https://www.ecmag.com/magazine/articles/article-detail/codes-standards-communications-systems-grounding-rules-article-800-provides-specific-requirements) — Grounding and bonding focus
- 🔗 [Mike Holt — Article 800 PDF (free)](https://www.mikeholt.com/instructor2/img/product/pdf/11LE-968-sample.pdf) — Study PDF sample from Mike Holt
- 🔗 [Mike Holt — Free Communications Systems PDF (Articles 800, 805, 810, 820)](https://www.mikeholt.com/newsletters.php?action=display&letterID=2536) — Free 2020 NEC-based PDF download
- 🔗 [MyNFPA70.com — Chapter 8 Overview](https://mynfpa70.com/mynfpa70welcome/chapter8/) — Chapter 8 article index

### Article 805 — Communications Circuits

Article 805 covers wiring methods, cable types, and installation requirements for communications circuits (voice, data, low-voltage signaling) from the service point to terminal equipment.

- 🔗 [MyNFPA70.com — Chapter 8 (Articles Index)](https://mynfpa70.com/mynfpa70welcome/chapter8/) — Includes Article 805 navigation
- 🔗 [Mike Holt — Free Communications PDF (includes 805)](https://www.mikeholt.com/newsletters.php?action=display&letterID=2536) — Covers 800/805/810/820 in one guide
- 🔗 [ElectricalLicenseRenewal.com — Article 800/805 Content](https://www.electricallicenserenewal.com/Electrical-Continuing-Education-Courses/NEC-Content.php?sectionID=975.0%2F1000) — CE course-style breakdown

### Article 810 — Antenna Systems

Article 810 covers antenna systems for radio and television reception, including grounding of masts, lead-in conductors, and satellite dishes.

- 🔗 [Mike Holt — Free Communications PDF (includes 810)](https://www.mikeholt.com/newsletters.php?action=display&letterID=2536)
- 🔗 [MyNFPA70.com — Chapter 8 Articles Index](https://mynfpa70.com/mynfpa70welcome/chapter8/)

### Article 820 — Community Antenna TV / Radio Distribution (CATV/Coax)

Article 820 covers coaxial cable systems used for distributing high-frequency signals — cable TV, CATV, CCTV, and broadband over coax.

- 🔗 [Scribd — NEC Articles 800, 810, 820 PDF](https://www.scribd.com/document/210389274/nec-vol-2-articulos-800-810-y-820-pdf) — Free read access
- 🔗 [Syston Cable — Communications Cables NEC Ratings PDF](https://www.systoncable.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Communications-Cables-NEC-Ratings-and-Transmission.pdf) — Cable ratings and Article 820 reference
- 🔗 [Mike Holt — Free Communications PDF (includes 820)](https://www.mikeholt.com/newsletters.php?action=display&letterID=2536)

### Article 830 — Network-Powered Broadband Communications

Article 830 covers network-powered broadband systems (e.g., fiber + power combined delivery systems from utility to premises).

- 🔗 [MyNFPA70.com — Chapter 8 Articles Index](https://mynfpa70.com/mynfpa70welcome/chapter8/)
- 🔗 [NFPA 70 Online (2023)](https://link.nfpa.org/all-publications/70/2023) — Free preview; full access with free account

### Article 840 — Premises-Powered Broadband Communications

Article 840 covers optical fiber-based broadband systems where power is supplied from the customer premises (e.g., ONT installations for fiber internet — directly relevant to FTTH/FTTx telecom work).

- 🔗 [MyNFPA70.com — Chapter 8 Articles Index](https://mynfpa70.com/mynfpa70welcome/chapter8/)
- 🔗 [NFPA 70 Online (2023)](https://link.nfpa.org/all-publications/70/2023)

## ⚙️ Recommended Anki Settings for NEC Study

### Optimized Settings (FSRS + V3 Scheduler)

| Setting | Recommended Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Scheduler | V3 (auto in modern Anki) | Required for FSRS |
| FSRS | Enabled | Best retention algorithm |
| Desired Retention | 90–95% | 95% = 2x workload; 90% is a solid balance |
| Learning Steps | `25m` | Single intraday step; FSRS-compatible |
| Relearning Steps | `10m` | For lapsed/forgotten cards |
| Graduating Interval | `3d` | First review interval after graduating |
| Easy Interval | `4d` | Skip-ahead interval for instant recalls |
| New Cards/Day | `9999` (or your pace) | Don't let Anki cap your flow |
| Maximum Reviews/Day | `9999` | Let FSRS manage the queue |

**Button Guide:** Press **Again** only when wrong. Press **Good** as your default correct answer. Use **Hard** sparingly for correct-but-difficult. Use **Easy** only when the card was instant and effortless.
